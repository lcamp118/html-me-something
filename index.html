My Three Dogs

Zeus

Hello! My name is Zeus and I am a 6 year old Rottweiler mix. My mom and dad adopted me from KC Pet Project. I am their first rescue and let's just say they loved me so much they just had to rescue even more animals!!

Not only am I the oldest, I am the alpha dog. I hog all the toys, food, and attention. I am also the *BEST* guard dog ever!! I bark or growl whenever I see anything, whether it is a squirrel or a kid walking down the street with their family. No one sneaks up on my family!!

But don't let that fool you! I only bark to see if you mean my family harm. In reality, I just love getting attention from people!! And I love to give kisses!!

Nala

My name is Nala. I am a purebred German Shepherd girl that my parents also adopted from KC Pet Project. I didn't have the best start in life. When KC Pet Project found me I was living at a different "animal rescue" who didn't quite understand how to properly care for a large amount of animals. They often did not let me out of my kennel so I had no choice but to go to the bathroom where I slept.

When my parents adopted me, they knew it would be a long hard road to getting me to my best self but they didn't care. They brought me home and started leash training me immediately. It was very weird to have a collar on with this leash from it, but my dad really helped me learn how to walk on a leash. It took several months for me to understand how much better it is NOT to go to the bathroom in my kennel.

I am now 5 years old and very happy!! I love my pet siblings!! We like to run around and wrestle outside. My favorite thing is when my mom gives me an ice cube to chew on in the morning!!

Snowflake

Hi I am Snowflake! I am a White German Shepherd mix that my parents adopted from KCK Animal Control. When my parents brought me home, I was very scared. My parents do not know much about my history because I was a stray but they think I may have been abused as a puppy. I was very timid and wouldn't come out from the corner. Also, I am terrified of brooms to this day.

With lots of love and patience and help from my doggy siblings I have really come out of my shell. In fact, I love to jump fences and several times my parents had to chase me and my doggy siblings down the street to bring us back home. Once I learned that I need to stay within my yard, I started learning how to dig holes. My parents don't like that either. Otherwise, I am a quiet dog who rarely barks. I don't give kisses like other dogs, instead, I like to jump up and "kiss" by pressing my nose to my mom's lips. We do this every morning!!
